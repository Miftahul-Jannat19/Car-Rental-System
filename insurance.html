3. What's Not Covered

  • Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Unauthorized drivers or off-road use
  • Damage to tires, glass, or underbody (unless part of an accident)
  • Personal belongings inside the car
  • Late reporting of incidents (after 24 hours)

5. In Case of Accident or Theft

  1. Call CARRUS support immediately.
  2. Contact the local police and obtain a report.
  3. Submit a claim report within 24 hours of the incident.
  4. Provide photo documentation if possible.

6. Deductibles and Excess
